Assi Ghat- The Peace of Hindu pilgrims.
Assi Ghat, located on the southern end of Varanasi along the banks of the sacred Ganges River, It is one of the most significant and bustling ghats in the city.
It is named after the Assi River, which meets the Ganges at this spot.
